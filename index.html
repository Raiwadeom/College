About Our Institution

Shivaji Mahavidyalaya is a recognized center for higher education, founded with a missionary zeal to advance educationally backward communities in the economically and socially developing region near the Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Andhra borders. The college offers 18 diverse academic programs across Arts, Commerce, Science, and a Computer-based professional course, enrolling over 1600 students in its regular program. With a large campus, dedicated and highly qualified faculty(including 27 Ph.D. holders and 7 research guides), and a strong focus on research and community service(NSS, NCC, Earn and Learn scheme), the institution is committed to providing quality, holistic education for the advancement and betterment of the masses, as reflected by its NAAC A+ grade accreditation.

College History

Starting with a handful of traditional courses, the college grew rapidly, recognizing the need for technical education. The introduction of the Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) and Bachelor of Computer Science (BCS) programs marked a significant milestone, aligning the institution with the demands of the modern workforce. We continue to adapt our curriculum to stay ahead of industry trends.

Governance and Administration

The college is governed by a dedicated board of management that ensures academic autonomy, financial stability, and adherence to regulatory guidelines. The Principal, supported by department heads and administrative staff, oversees the day-to-day operations.

Key Governing Bodies

  • Management Council: Provides strategic direction and financial oversight.
  • Academic Council: Responsible for curriculum design, teaching methods, and academic quality assurance.
  • Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC):Monitors and evaluates the overall institutional performance.

Organizational Structure

The structure promotes transparency and accountability, ensuring all decisions are student-centric. The BCA and BCS departments report directly to the Principal, facilitating quick implementation of program improvements and initiatives.

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

Program Overview

The BCA program is a three-year, full-time undergraduate course designed to provide students with a strong theoretical and practical foundation in computer applications. It prepares students for careers in software development, system management, and IT consulting.

Duration

3 Years (6 Semesters)

Eligibility

H.S.C. (10+2) or equivalent

Intake

60 Seats

Career Prospects

  • Software Developer/Engineer
  • Web Developer
  • Database Administrator
  • System Analyst
  • Network Administrator

Bachelor of Computer Science (BCS)

Program Overview

The BCS program is an intensive three-year degree focusing on the theoretical foundations of computer science, mathematics, and programming. It is ideal for students aiming for advanced studies (like MCA/MSc) or research-oriented roles.

Duration

3 Years (6 Semesters)

Eligibility

H.S.C. (10+2) or equivalent

Intake

60 Seats

Career Prospects

  • Higher Education (MCA, MSc IT/CS)
  • Software Tester/QA Analyst
  • Data Scientist (Entry Level)
  • IT Trainer/Educator
  • Research Assistant

Admission Process (2024-25)

Follow these simple steps to secure your admission into the BCA or BCS program for the academic year 2024-25:

1

Online Pre-Registration

Fill out the mandatory online pre-registration form. Upload necessary preliminary documents (Mark sheets, ID Proof).

Go to Pre-Registration Portal
2

Document Verification & Counseling

Visit the college for physical document verification and a short counseling session with the HOD.

3

Fee Payment

Pay the required academic fees (can be done online or offline at the college counter).

4

Confirmation & Enrollment

Receive the final admission confirmation letter and your student ID, marking your official enrollment.

Required Documents:

  • 10th and 12th Mark Sheet (Original and 2 copies)
  • Leaving Certificate/Transfer Certificate (Original)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Gap Certificate (if applicable)
  • Aadhaar Card and 4 Passport Size Photos

Academic Curricula & Subjects

BCA Curriculum Structure (CBCS)

  • Year I (Sem I & II):

    Introduction to Programming (C), Discrete Mathematics, Digital Electronics, Communication Skills, Operating System Fundamentals.

  • Year II (Sem III & IV):

    Data Structures, Object-Oriented Programming (C++), DBMS, Computer Networks, Software Engineering, Linux Administration.

  • Year III (Sem V & VI):

    Web Technology (PHP/Python), Java Programming, Data Science Fundamentals, Cloud Computing, Project Work & Internships.

BCS Curriculum Structure (CBCS)

  • Year I (Sem I & II):

    C Programming, Statistical Methods, Calculus, Computer Organization, Logic and Switching Theory.

  • Year II (Sem III & IV):

    Advanced Data Structures, Python Programming, Database Management Systems, Computer Graphics, Operations Research.

  • Year III (Sem V & VI):

    Advanced Java, Web Development, Software Testing, Distributed Computing, Research Project.

Electives:

Students can choose from a range of skill-based and general electives, including courses on Mobile Application Development, Cyber Security, and Tally/Accounting.
